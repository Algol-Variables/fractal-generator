This is a simple Mandelbrot and Julia Set generator implemented in JavaScript.

  • Click to zoom in.
  • Click and drag to zoom into drawn area.
  • Ctrl + Click to zoom out.
  • Shift + Click to centre at current coordinates.
  • Alt(Option) + Click to toggle between Mandelbrot and Julia modes at the current coordinates.
  • Click 'R' to reset to default settings.
    • Click 'I' to zoom in at current coordinate. Click 'O' to zoom out at current coordinate.
  • Click 'M' to cycle through modes.
  • Click 'J' to enter Julia mode at current coordinates.
  • Click 'V' to cycle through variants.
  • Click 'T' to cycle through color themes.
  • Click '↑/↓' to shift color theme up or down.
  • Click '←/→' to rotate Julia Set clockwise or anti-clockwise.
  • Click '⟳' to turn 'Julia spin' animation on or off.
  • Click 'E' to toggle exponent (2 or 3).
