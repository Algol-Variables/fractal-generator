How to use Fractal Generator.

Click Zoom in at mouse location.
Click and drag Zoom into drawn area.
Ctrl + Click Zoom out.
Shift + Click Centre at current coordinates.
Alt(Option) + Click Toggle between Mandelbrot and Julia modes at the current coordinates.
Click 'R' Reset to default settings.
Click 'I' Zoom in at current coordinate.
Click 'O' Zoom out at current coordinate.
Click 'Z' Start or stop zoom animation at current coordinate.
Click 'M' Cycle through modes.
Click 'V' Cycle through variants.
Click 'E' Cycle exponent (2-6).
Click 'T' Cycle through color themes.
Click '↑/↓' Shift color theme up or down.
Click 'J' Enter Julia mode at current coordinates.
Click '←/→' Rotate Julia Set clockwise or anti-clockwise.
Click ⟳ Start or stop 'spinning Julia' animation.
